Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Mood Fixer help center! Here you can find answers to how our platform works, who we serve, and how we aim to change your day for the better.

Mood Fixer is an interactive, AI-driven website designed to instantly shift your mindset. our mission is to deliver the exact words you need right when you need them—whether that is a fascinating fact, a powerful dose of motivation, or a well-timed joke. We do not believe in boring walls of text or dull colors. We deliver high-energy, impactful results in just one or two sentences.

Our platform is inclusive and open to absolutely everyone. We cater to all individuals across the globe, including:

  • Females
  • Males
  • Transgender individuals

No matter who you are, your emotional well-being matters to us.

We make it incredibly easy for you to share your feelings. When you visit the site, you can either click an emoji that represents how you feel right now or type a quick description of what is on your mind.

Our built-in AI analyzes your input to instantly detect your current state—whether you are sad, happy, just waking up, getting ready for sleep, or simply having a normal day. It then handpicks the perfect response to match your energy.

We believe that a single phrase can change a person's life, day, week, or even year. Long paragraphs can be boring and tiresome to read. By keeping our results to a maximum of two sentences, we ensure that the message is powerful, memorable, and gives you an instant boost of morale before you leave our site.

Mood Fixer features an active calendar and clock system. We save your sign-in date and time so you can track your journey with us. This system is also designed to encourage you to return for your second day, helping you build a daily habit of checking in on your happiness.
