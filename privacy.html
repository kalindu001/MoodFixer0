Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Welcome to Mood Fixer. Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site to find motivation, facts, and jokes.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best "Mood Fix" possible, we collect the following information:

  • User Input: Any descriptions you type or emojis you click to express your mood.
  • Profile Details: Information regarding your gender (male, female, or transgender) to help personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: We save the date and time you sign in to help you track your progress using our built-in calendar.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Personalize Content: Our AI analyzes your mood and description to provide the most relevant one-to-two sentence motivation, fact, or joke.
  • Improve the System: Your interactions help us train our AI to be more accurate and helpful.
  • Future Development: We use general data to plan future updates and new features for the website.

3. AI and Data Processing

Our website uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to understand your emotional state quickly. This process is automated to ensure you get an instant result that can change your day or week for the better.

4. Data Retention

We save your sign-in history and calendar data so that when you return for your "second-day visit," the system recognizes your journey. You have the right to know your history and how much time you have spent improving your morale with us.

5. Security and Liability

While we strive to protect your data, please remember:

  • Personal Safety: Mood Fixer is for motivational purposes. We are not responsible for any personal injury, accidents, or self-harm resulting from your personal actions.
  • Professional Help: If you are in a crisis, please seek professional medical assistance immediately.

6. Updates to This Policy

As Mood Fixer is not "finished" and will receive future updates, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features. We encourage you to check this page regularly.
