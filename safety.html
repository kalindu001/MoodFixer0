Safety & Crisis Help

You Matter. Please Reach Out.

🆘 Immediate Help Needed?

If you are in immediate danger, please call your local emergency services right away.



At Mood Fixer, our main goal is to bring a smile to your face and motivation to your day. However, we know that some days are too heavy for a quick joke or a motivational quote to fix. If you are going through an overwhelming crisis, experiencing thoughts of self-harm, or feeling like you cannot go on, please know that you do not have to carry this pain alone.

While our AI tools are here to lift your spirits, Mood Fixer is not a crisis intervention service or a substitute for professional mental health care. Your safety is the most important thing to us. People care about you, and there is real support available right now.

Immediate Crisis Resources

If you are in immediate danger of hurting yourself or someone else, please use the resources below. These services are free, confidential, and available 24/7:

🚨 National Emergency Services

Call your local emergency number (such as 911, 112, 199, or 999 depending on your country) or visit the nearest hospital emergency room.

🌍 International Crisis Lines

If you are outside of Sri Lanka, please find free, immediate support available in your country via Find A Helpline or Befrienders Worldwide.
